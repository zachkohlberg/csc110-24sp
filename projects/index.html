CSC110 Projects

Projects Overview

This book contains all of the projects for CSC110. The projects are organized into chapters by topic (Project 1: Character Sheet, Project 2: Dice Game, Project 3: Connect Four, and the Final Project. They can also be grouped by grade contract:

  • The C contract requires all guided projects:
    • Guided Project 1.1
    • Guided Project 1.2
    • Guided Project 2.1
    • Guided Project 2.2
  • The B contract requires the expanded projects in addition to the guided projects:
    • Expanded Project 1.3
    • Expanded Project 2.3
  • The A contract requires the final project in addition to the guided and expanded projects:
    • Final Project

There is also a page that describes how to propose a modified project. This is relevant if you would like to replace one of the expanded projects, or in rare cases replace the guided projects.

Note: Projects 3.1 and 3.2 have been cut from the required work for this semester. I will still post those assignments when they are ready, but they have no impact on your final grade whether you complete or skip them.
