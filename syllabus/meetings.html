CSC110 Syllabus

Class Meetings

In the event that I cannot attend a class in person, I will usually hold class remotely via Zoom or find another faculty member to cover the meeting for me. If I need to cancel class, then I will notify everyone as early as possible in a Blackboard announcement.

Class Structure

The course schedule will have the agenda for each class meeting, and all activities and material from class will be posted to Blackboard. If you want me to cover a specific topic or spend more time on something, then let me know and I will try to fit it into the agenda for an upcoming class.

I will try to include a mix of group activities, programming demos, and more traditional lectures during class. You should show up to each class prepared for what we're doing that day, which means completing any reading or other homework assignments before class starts. It's not fair to the people you're working with if you don't know what's going on, and you won't get as much out of a lecture or demonstration if you skipped the reading.

Attendance Policy

You should attend class whenever possible, but you do not need my permission to miss class or leave early. You also don't need to explain every absence to me, although I would appreciate knowing what's going on if you have to miss multiple classes in a short period of time.

If you are feeling sick or suspect you may have COVID, the flu, or another contagious illness, then please stay home and focus on recovering. You will be fine if you miss a few classes, and you're not likely to get much out of class if you are not feeling well. Attending class while sick also poses a risk to everyone else.
